How to use AIS Running Total



Enter Arrival time in hours and minutes
EX: Your tee time "

The hours entered must be a positive number or zero (0).
EX: subtract your travel time- Look it up in Waze App

The minutes entered must be a positive number or zero (0).
EX: Maybe subtract 30 minutes to use driving range, putt, and get a beer

Select "add" or "subtract"
EX: Maybe subtract time for the bathroom before you play

Click "Click to Calculate" button. The AIS Running Total will appear.
EX: This is your AIS to leave your house

To clear the AIS Running Total click "Reset".

If you have added the wrong number to the AIS Running Total, you can re-enter the wrong number and then select "subtract".
